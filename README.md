A **full featured cryptocurrency trading simulator** designed for practice and portfolio projects.  
This dashboard allows you to:

- View live 24-hour price charts

- Calculate total profit and loss (P/L)

## ðŸ”¹ Features

1. **Real-time Prices**  
   Prices are fetched from the free [CoinGecko API](https://www.coingecko.com/en/api) and updated every 10 seconds.

2. **Multi-Coin Support**  
   Switch between BTC, ETH, and SOL to trade and track different assets.

3. **Interactive Chart**  
   Live 24-hour line chart using [Chart.js](https://www.chartjs.org/).

4. **Buy/Sell Simulator**  
   Enter an amount in USD to buy or sell your selected cryptocurrency.  
   All trades are **simulated** with fake money.

5. **Profit/Loss Calculation**  
   Displays total P/L in real-time based on your holdings and balance.

6. **Trade History**  
   Records all your transactions and keeps them in **LocalStorage**.

7. **Dark/Light Mode**  
   Toggle theme and save preference using LocalStorage.
